Our Story

At Tani Kopi, we blend rural charm with premium coffee sophistication. Founded with a passion for exceptional coffee, we source our beans from top local farmers dedicated to quality and sustainability. Our cozy atmosphere makes it the perfect spot to relax and enjoy every sip.

Our mission is to create a community where every visit feels like a warm embrace. With friendly service, expertly crafted beverages, and a genuine love for coffee, we believe that great coffee brings people together, making each cup a memorable part of your day.

Tani Kopi offers a cozy and welcoming space, perfect for students.

Enjoy our comfortable seating, free Wi-Fi, and a quiet atmosphere ideal for studying, meeting friends, or taking a break with a delicious cup of coffee.

We believe in creating a welcoming space where every cup of coffee brings people together and fosters a sense of community.

Ozik Putra Jarwo, "Director of Tani Kopi
